Character Development Workbook

Grow Your Character, One Week at a Time

Student Workbook Preview

The AniMottos Workbook helps children explore timeless values through stories, journaling, art, and reflection. Each animal mentor invites students to grow in curiosity, creativity, responsibility, courage, honesty, and integrity—learning not just what these values mean, but how to live them every day. Along the way, children gain the confidence to express their character with pride and share who they’re becoming with the world.
